Julie Carter

Administrative Program Coordinator

As Administrative Program Coordinator for Cleveland Clinic Ventures (CCV), Julie develops, implements, manages and maintains the operational systems and activities of CCV. She also functions as the administrative, technical and operational resource for the CCV team.  

Julie has worked at Cleveland Clinic for 5 years, previously holding a role in Innovations with the Global Cardiovascular Innovation Center where she assisted with coordinating efforts within GCIC’s Incubator/Accelerator Program and provided support to the tenants.
